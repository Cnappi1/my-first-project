Get Crafty

This Week's Craft.

As we get into the warmer months, it is important to enjoy some time outside! You body thrives on sunshine. Here is a fun outdoor craft that we really hope you will share your results with us.

This week's craft is a simple one, but we hope it bring you think back fondly on your childhood like it did with us. We want our Happy At Home family to go outside and draw something beautiful with sidewalk chalk. Draw a garden, a chubby cat, or an abstract design all your own.
The only rules for this craf are enjoy the sunshine, the experience, and share the results with us by tagging us on social media.
We can't wait to see what you create!

Find your inner artist!

Craft Tips of the Week.

  • Pick one craft at time. It is so easy to get caught up in the exciting world and vast options of the craft world. This can also cause you to become overwhelmed with lots of unfinished projects and a struggling bank account. The key is to find the craft you are wanting to try the most and see it through. This will give you the satisfaction of having an end result you love that had your full attention.
  • You are not a professional. You crafts may not turn out as great as the examples provided by the person posting the directions for your craft. That doesn't mean what you created is any less special or worthy of feeling accomplished for creating it. Did you have fun making it? That is all that matters. One of the main points of crafting or creating art is to enjoy yourself not just the item you are creating.
