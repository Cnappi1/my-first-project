How about a quick game?

We are just really feeling nostalgic lately because this week's card game is a favorite from many people's childhoods.
We have been playing the card game Crazy Eights again!
For those of you who haven't had the pleasure of playing this game before, we have included the instructions below!

  • All you need is a standard deck of cards.
  • Shuffle your deck of cards. Deal 5 cards to each player. If only 2 people are playing, you will deal 7 cards to each player.
  • Place the remaining cards face down to form a draw pile, and flip the top card over to start the discard pile.
  • Each player takes turns playing a card from their hand that matches the rank or suit of the top card on the discard pile. If a player cannot play a card, they must draw from the draw pile. Eights are wild and can be played at any time, allowing the player to change the current suit.
  • The goal is to be the first player to get rid of all your cards.
