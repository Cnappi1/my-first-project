The Midnight Feast By: Lucy Foley Mystery/Suspence It is no secret here that we love a book written from the point of view of multiple characters. We also love a good mystery/suspense. This one kept us guessing and on the edge of our seats with every turn of the page. Full of likeable and unlikeable characters all with their on thoughts of what's going on throughout the story, this one is a must read! 4.8/5

Can't wait for the next book review release! - Cindy W.
