This Week's Beginner Friendly Meal Is...

Have you ever been craving the cheesy melty layers of a lasgana, but you just don't have the time, energy or desire to create it from scratch? Well do we have an easy and delcious recipe for you!
This week's recipe is a ravioli bake.
Lazy Lasagna:
Ingredients:
25 Oz Frozen Ravioli
24 OZ Jar of Your Favorite Pasta Sauce
2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
1/3 cup grated parmesan

Directions:

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  • Grease 9x13 baking dish
  • Spread 3/4 cup of sauce onto the bottom of your dish. (This keeps your pasta from sticking to the bottom and burning)
  • Arange half the the ravioli in a single layer on top of the sauce
  • Cover the ravioli with half the remaining sauce and half of the mozzarella cheese
  • Use the rest of your ravioli to form another single layer, then the rest of your sauce and finally the rest of your mozzarella cheese and the parmesan cheese.
  • Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil after 30 minutes and bake for another 15. This will let your cheese fishin melting.
  • Cool for 10 minutes then ENJOY!
