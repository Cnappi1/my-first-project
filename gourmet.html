This Week's Gourmet Recipe Is...

Have you seen those roast potatoes all over the internet that are crispy on the outside and absolutely fluffy on the inside. Well we have and we had to figure out how to make them. This recipe will be perfect along side roast chicken recipe from a few weeks ago.
Roast Potatoes:
Ingredients:
2 Pounds of Yukon Gold or Russett
2 Table Spoons Baking Sode
Good Quality High Heat Oil (We used Avocado Oil)
Salt
Seasonings of Your Choice. (We used garlic and rosemary)

Directions:

  • Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  • Peel the potatoes and dice them into 2 inch chunks
  • Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Once the water starts boiling add in your 2 tablespoons of baking soda.
  • Add in your chunked potatoes and boil until fork tender but not fully cooked.
  • Drain your potatoes well. Tossing to rough up the outside of them slightly. This helps give that cripsy outside and softer inside.
  • While the potatoes are on the baking sheet, get a sause pan and heat 4-5 tablespoons of your choosen oil. Also mix in your desired seasonings to flavor the oil.
  • Once the potaoes have rested for 15-20 minutes drizzle and combine them well with your heated oil.
  • Roast your potatoes in the oven for 30-40 minutes. Flipping them around 15-20 minutes.
  • Remove the potatoes from the oven. Sprinkle with fresh herbs or desired toppings and server. Enjoy!
