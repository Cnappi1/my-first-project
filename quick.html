Have you ever been craving the flavor combinations of a s'more, but you just don't have the desire to play with fire?

The perfect snack for a movie night or binge-watching a TV show

Smore's Snack Mix:
Ingredients:
1 Box Honey Teddy Grahams
1 Bag Mini Marshmallows
8 Oz Milk Chocolate Chips

Directions: (It won't ever get any simpler than this!)
Dump all ingredients into a large mixing bowl and mix well with a spoon or spatula.
